Chapter 25: Emerald Sphere


“We need to exercise caution. We know little about level 4 zombies, and my fire shield is nearly depleted,” Lucas cautioned, his jian sword cutting through the approaching zombies.


“Swoosh!” His swift slashes dispatched the undead before him.


“Lucy, stick close to me,” he instructed, despite the continuous sounds of their leveling up. The ongoing battle felt like an eternity to them.


“Lucas, I've found it!” Lucy whispered urgently behind him. She gestured to the north of the woman's house, where a distinctive zombie was surrounded by a horde of others.


“Why is it so far from where we're fighting?” Lucas wondered aloud, taken aback by the distance of the level 4 zombie.


“Lucy, we need to reach it. The shield is fading,” he decided, seizing her hand and leading her in a run towards the distant target. Amid the frenzy, he paid little heed to the discrepancy between their noise and the zombie's position.


“Ahh!!” Lucy exclaimed suddenly, stumbling due to Lucas's abrupt movements and rapid pace.


“Slow down, Lucas! Please!” she implored, struggling to catch her breath as he continued to pull her forward with little respite.


“Why do you always drag me without a word of explanation?” she chastised, her frustration evident.


The level 4 zombie seemed to sense their approach, emitting an agitated shriek.


“Grrr!” It charged towards them. While such a reaction from zombies wasn't unprecedented, this one appeared… vexed.


“Lucy, freeze it!” Lucas directed, gauging the zombie's rapid speed.


“Freeze!” Lucy invoked, channeling the power from her earring towards the zombie in accordance with Lucas's guidance. Sensing his intent, she hoped for an effective strategy.


As the zombie began to freeze and slow, Lucas closed in swiftly, his jian sword aimed at the creature. He muttered a command, “Fire blaze!” slashing his blade through the developing ice, terminating the zombie's life.


“Lucy, use icicle on its head!” Lucas shouted, his sword pointing toward the frozen zombie's cranium


“It's already dead. icicle is my last ability,” Lucy replied, her actions swift as she beheaded another zombie.


“Just do it, trust me!” Lucas urged, urgency in his voice as he drove his sword further.


“Alright! Icicle!” Lucy declared, determination in her eyes. She directed her intent toward the specific point where the icicle would penetrate.


“Swoosh!” An icicle materialized before Lucy, soaring through the air and piercing the zombie's head, creating a small hole in its forehead. Instantly, the zombie's brain was encased in ice.


Seeing the icicle embedded in the zombie's forehead, Lucas swiftly brought down his jian sword, severing the decaying head from its body.
