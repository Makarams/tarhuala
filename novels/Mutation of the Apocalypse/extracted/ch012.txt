Chapter 12: The Appraisal Magnifier


Upon entering the store, Lucas surveyed the surroundings, finding no signs of zombies present. With a relieved exhale, he settled down on the ground next to Lucy, who had already taken a seat. Finally, having a moment to check their notifications, Lucas began the conversation.


“It seems I took down 17 level 1 zombies and 2 level 2 zombies. How about you?” he inquired.


Lucy, still catching her breath, quickly accessed her notifications. “I managed to eliminate twelve level 1 zombies. I've reached level 5.” Grateful for Lucas's assistance, Lucy recognized that she had been able to eliminate a considerable number of zombies due to his distraction.


Lucas advised, “You should invest all your stat points in strength.” Muttering his instruction, he allocated four points to his own strength.

“Huh?” Lucas exclaimed; his gaze fixated on the newly acquired items.


“Lucas, what's going on? Did you complete another achievement?” Lucy asked, her curiosity piqued.


Lucas nodded, sharing, “This is my third completed achievement. It appears I've already taken down approximately forty level 1 zombies and four level 2 zombies... around four groups of zombies.”


Lucy was taken aback. “You've eliminated that many?”


Lucas acknowledged her amazement with a subtle grin. “Yes, well, I am determined to gain strength.”


Lucy's internal monologue chimed in, “Of course, he's accumulated this many kills. He's truly driven to increase his strength.”


Observing the rewards, Lucy commented, “So, you received a silver ring and a magnifier? Are they even useful?”


Lucas clarified, “Indeed, they are useful.” He picked up the magnifier, explaining its purpose. “This is called an appraisal magnifier. Do you understand the term 'appraisal'?”


“No, I'm not familiar with that word at all.”


“Understandable. It's not a common term. In the context of games, it refers to a skill that allows you to detect or unveil the properties of an item.”


Lucas then picked up the Hurricane Ring, a seemingly ordinary piece of jewelry with a slightly thicker band and a wing symbol engraved on it.


“Do you see this symbol? Let's examine it together,” Lucas instructed, moving the magnifier over the ring.

Lucy's excitement overflowed. “Oh my God, yes! That's a supernatural ability!”


Lucas maintained his calm demeanor. “Yes, well, considering our current situation, such things should be less surprising. After all, we're navigating an apocalyptic world with unknown, advanced technology.”


Lucy regained her composure, realizing her overly enthusiastic response. “You're right, apologies for my overreaction.”


Lucas concluded their discussion. “We should rest here for a few hours before using the ability. I can activate it once per day.”


Shifting gears, Lucas addressed their immediate needs. “Are you hungry?” He retrieved his canned food and stated.

“Everyone needs to eat to live. You don't have to be afraid to admit that.”

“I'm glad you're feeling better now. My stamina seems to have recovered. Let's leave this place,” she suggested, aware that Lucas had extended their rest due to her condition, not his own.
