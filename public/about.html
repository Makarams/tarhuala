The Author

Tarhuala

Business analyst by day and reader by night, I have spent more time since childhood with fictional characters than the real people around me.

My pen name, Tarhuala, means “a thread of words.” It weaves together tar (Persian/Urdu for thread), hua (Mandarin for speech), and la (Spanish/French) to represent language as the fabric of stories, worlds, and voices.

I write because the human mind is strange, beautiful, and unsettling in ways worth exploring. That is why I focus on horror, survival, and the uncanny.

If I had one wish, it would be that the world outside is not as gray as it sometimes feels.

— Tarhuala
