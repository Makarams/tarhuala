The Author

Tarhuala

A software engineer who spends his days solving problems that shouldn't exist and his nights reading about worlds that don't.

Plays violin. Watches too much drama. Has thought more deeply about fictional characters than about his own life decisions, and has made peace with that.

Writes because the human mind is capable of imagining things so beautiful and so terrible that it feels wasteful not to put them somewhere. Horror, survival, fantasy, the strange and the dark — all of it lives in the same place.

This is that place.

If I had one wish, it would be that the world outside is not as gray as it looks.

— Tarhuala
